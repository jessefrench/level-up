CHANGE TODAY FOR A BETTER TOMMORROW

At Level Up, local youth cultivate determination and perseverance through Mixed Martial Arts. They learn to apply these values on and off the mat in the pursuit of thier ambitions through our Student Trainer Certification and College Access programs.

WHY

In addition to athletics, Level Up provides college access and career opportunities to local youth. Our young people are exposed to a disproportionately high level of poverty and crime, and live in neighborhoods that experience a greater prevalence of obesity-related illnesses. From assistance with college applications to employment at our gym, access to our weekly nutrition workshops, and free athletic training, Level Up strives to meet the needs of our youth in an authentic and compassionate manner.
